SCHNEIDER ELECTRIC ECOSTRUXURE GRID
PRODUCT AND SOLUTION OVERVIEW FOR DISTRIBUTION UTILITIES
VERSION 2024

SECTION 1 - ECOSTRUXURE GRID PLATFORM OVERVIEW

1.1 Platform Architecture
EcoStruxure Grid is Schneider Electric's IoT enabled, open and interoperable
architecture and platform for electrical distribution utilities. The platform
spans three layers:
- Connected Products    : Smart sensors, meters, protection relays, switchgear
- Edge Control         : Substation automation, feeder automation, DMS
- Apps, Analytics and Services: ADMS, AMI analytics, asset health management

1.2 Core Software Components
- EcoStruxure Power Monitoring Expert (PME): Power quality and energy management
- EcoStruxure Advanced Distribution Management System (ADMS): Grid operations
- EcoStruxure Grid Asset Advisor: Predictive asset health monitoring
- EcoStruxure Microgrid Advisor: Microgrid energy optimisation
- EcoStruxure Energy Hub: Prosumer energy management and flexibility

SECTION 2 - ADMS CAPABILITIES FOR RENEWABLE INTEGRATION

2.1 Distributed Energy Resource Management (DERM)
EcoStruxure ADMS includes a native DERM module that provides:
- Real time visibility of all DG resources on the distribution network
- Forecasting engine for solar and wind generation (15 minute resolution)
- Optimal dispatch of flexible resources to manage voltage and congestion
- VVC (Volt VAR Control) with DG reactive power control capability
- Automated restoration considering DG availability post fault

2.2 Volt VAR Optimisation (VVO)
The VVO module in ADMS continuously optimises voltage profiles by:
- Coordinating capacitor banks, LTC transformers, and SVR settings
- Using DG inverter reactive power as a control resource
- Targeting voltage within plus or minus 3 percent of nominal
- Reducing distribution losses by 2 to 5 percent typically

2.3 Outage Management System (OMS) with DG Awareness
- Islanding detection and management for DG equipped feeders
- Safe restoration sequences considering DG status
- Crew dispatch considering DG work permits
- Customer notification with DG impact consideration

SECTION 3 - FEEDER AUTOMATION FOR HIGH DG PENETRATION

3.1 Recloser and Sectionaliser Automation
For feeders with DG penetration above 30 percent:
- Directional overcurrent protection required (bidirectional power flow)
- Recloser dead time adjustment to allow DG to trip before reclose
- Recloser settings: Recommended minimum 2 second dead time with DG
- Feeder automation with SCADA connectivity to ADMS

3.2 Recommended Schneider Electric Products for High DG Feeders
- PRA200 Recloser Controller: Directional protection, IEC 61850 native
- Easergy P3 Protection Relay: Advanced directional overcurrent and earth fault
- Easergy T300 RTU: IEC 61850 and DNP3 communication for feeder automation
- PowerLogic ION9000: Revenue grade metering at DG PCC

3.3 Feeder F-7 Indiranagar Upgrade Recommendation
Based on the BESCOM solar forecast showing Feeder F-7 reaching 47 percent
DG penetration by 2026, the following upgrades are recommended:
- Replace existing overcurrent relay with Easergy P3 directional protection
- Install PRA200 recloser at mid-feeder point for sectionalisation
- Add PowerLogic ION9000 meter at substation feeder head
- Integrate with ADMS for real time monitoring and VVO
- Estimated upgrade cost: Rs 28 lakh per feeder (excluding civil works)
- Estimated timeline: 3 to 4 months per feeder

SECTION 4 - ECOSTRUXURE GRID ASSET ADVISOR

4.1 Distribution Transformer Health Monitoring
Asset Advisor connects to smart sensors on distribution transformers to provide:
- Real time temperature monitoring (winding and oil temperature)
- Load current monitoring and overload prediction
- Dissolved gas analysis (DGA) trend monitoring via external sensors
- Remaining useful life estimation based on thermal ageing model
- Maintenance work order generation integrated with CMMS

4.2 Supported IoT Sensors for Distribution Assets
- EcoStruxure Transformer Expert: Vibration, temperature, partial discharge
- PowerLogic PM5000 series: Energy and power quality at substation level
- Easergy P3 relay: Protection event logging and fault recording
- SM6 Ring Main Unit with Smart Module: LV and MV switching status

SECTION 5 - MICROGRID AND PROSUMER SOLUTIONS

5.1 EcoStruxure Microgrid Advisor
For areas with high DG penetration requiring islanded operation:
- Energy management across solar, storage, and load
- Seamless transition between grid connected and island mode
- Optimise self consumption and minimise grid export
- Supports VPP aggregation for demand response programs

5.2 EcoStruxure Energy Hub
For DISCOM prosumer programs:
- Individual prosumer portal for energy monitoring and billing
- Aggregated flexibility from residential solar and storage
- API integration with DISCOM billing and ADMS systems
- Support for net metering, gross metering, and peer to peer trading
