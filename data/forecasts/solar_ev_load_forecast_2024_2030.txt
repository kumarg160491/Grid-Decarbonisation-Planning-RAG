SECTION 3 - COMBINED IMPACT ANALYSIS

3.1 Net Load Curve Impact
Solar generation during 10AM to 3PM is expected to push net load to near zero
or negative on high solar days by 2026 on commercial feeders.
Duck curve phenomenon expected in BESCOM area from 2025 onwards.

3.2 Morning EV Ramp Rate
EV charging combined with morning commercial load will create a 35 percent
steeper ramp rate between 7AM and 9AM by 2026.
Flexible demand response programs must be operational before 2025.

3.3 Storage Requirement
To manage the duck curve and EV evening peak:
- 800 MWh of grid scale BESS needed by 2026
- 200 MWh of distribution level BESS at 11kV substations
- 1,200 MWh of behind the meter storage by 2028
