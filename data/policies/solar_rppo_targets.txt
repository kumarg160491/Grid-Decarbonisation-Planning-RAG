RENEWABLE PURCHASE OBLIGATION (RPO) AND
RENEWABLE ENERGY CERTIFICATE FRAMEWORK
MINISTRY OF NEW AND RENEWABLE ENERGY - 2023

1. RPO TARGETS FOR DISTRIBUTION LICENSEES

1.1 Solar RPO Trajectory
Year 2023-24: 8.0 percent of total energy consumption from solar
Year 2024-25: 10.5 percent of total energy consumption from solar
Year 2025-26: 13.0 percent of total energy consumption from solar
Year 2026-27: 15.5 percent of total energy consumption from solar
Year 2027-28: 18.0 percent of total energy consumption from solar
Year 2028-29: 20.5 percent of total energy consumption from solar
Year 2029-30: 23.0 percent of total energy consumption from solar

1.2 Non-Solar RPO Trajectory
Year 2023-24: 11.5 percent from non-solar renewables (wind, hydro, biomass)
Year 2024-25: 13.5 percent from non-solar renewables
Year 2025-26: 15.0 percent from non-solar renewables
Year 2030-31: 21.0 percent from non-solar renewables

1.3 Total RPO including Solar
Year 2023-24: 19.5 percent total RPO
Year 2029-30: 43.33 percent total RPO (alignment with 500 GW target)

2. COMPLIANCE MECHANISM

2.1 Renewable Energy Certificates (REC)
- Solar REC floor price   : Rs 1,000 per MWh
- Solar REC ceiling price : Rs 1,500 per MWh
- Non-solar REC floor     : Rs 500 per MWh
- Non-solar REC ceiling   : Rs 1,000 per MWh
- REC validity period     : 1 year from issuance

2.2 RPO Compliance Options for DISCOMs
Option 1: Direct procurement via Power Purchase Agreement (PPA) with RE generators
Option 2: Purchase of RECs from exchange (IEX or PXIL)
Option 3: Captive rooftop solar generation counted toward solar RPO
Option 4: Open access renewable procurement by large consumers (offset RPO obligation)

2.3 Non-Compliance Penalty
- Penalty for RPO shortfall: Rs 1,500 per MWh for solar
- Penalty for non-solar shortfall: Rs 1,000 per MWh
- Penalty collected by SERC and used for renewable energy promotion fund

3. ROOFTOP SOLAR CONTRIBUTION TO RPO

3.1 Counting Methodology
- Net metered rooftop solar: 100 percent counted toward DISCOM solar RPO
- Gross metered rooftop solar: Only exported energy counted toward RPO
- Group captive rooftop: Counted toward consuming entity RPO obligation

3.2 DISCOM Incentive for Rooftop Promotion
DISCOMs achieving rooftop solar target of 10 percent of total connections:
- Additional 0.5 percent incentive on RPO compliance calculation
- Eligible for additional RDSS grant of Rs 50 crore per state DISCOM

4. GREEN ENERGY OPEN ACCESS

4.1 Eligibility
- Open access for renewable energy: Consumers above 100 kW (reduced from 1 MW)
- Banking of green energy: Permitted within state, monthly settlement
- Cross subsidy surcharge: 50 percent waiver for RE open access consumers
- Wheeling charges: As per SERC order, typically 10 to 15 percent of base tariff

4.2 Green Tariff Options
DISCOMs must offer green tariff options to consumers:
- Green Standard: 100 percent renewable energy supply at 10 percent premium
- Green Plus: 100 percent RE plus 1 REC per MWh at 15 percent premium
- Carbon Neutral Package: RE plus offset certificates at 20 percent premium
